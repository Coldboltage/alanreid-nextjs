### Cardsbolt update

Updating based upon the shop and then labelling each shop based upon difficulity was a good idea. There's times when it's more than fine to blast a site with new browsers so to not get rate limited. There's times where that strategy has no hope in hell to work, and reverting to a single browser process which humanises the process as well as possible, is the only method. Took a while to get to the point of creating the first part of this new shift, but I'm able to scan problematic websites just enough. There's a few tests I need to do but for now, it's a good add.
