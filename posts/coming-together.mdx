### Boi this burns a lot of tokens

There will be a point where I get an AI Agent to help with some things automatically but right now, I noticed with more and more products being added, the time it takes to go through every single shopProduct to get links, can be long. I was fairly surprised it took about an hour and while this process is very unlikely to happen, it does mean with the hardware I currently have with the amount of products, if I add about 23 times the amount of shopProducts than what I have now, which is currently 10,775, so that would be 230,000 shopProducts, I will exceed capacity for my setup and would probably be a bottleneck. To add, I was going through 7000 input tokens per second and 400 output tokens per second. that's 604 million input tokens and 34 million output tokens. I'm not too sure about the electricity cost but there might be a time I would need to upgrade the models I'm using. Right now, I see no reason to do so as Qwen3 4b Instruct 2507 does a good enough job and while there is some work to do with the candidate pages and stuff like that, after it's done, it's pretty simple. I just find it mad the amount of tokens I can go through in a day in the future.

### Finding products way easier than Google

While Google is a staple, I am dead honest when I say, it's easier to find products with Cardsbolt. You look for the name and if you see it, a listing exists. Here's the thing, I'm actually having a hard time finding products which are hard to get, on Google. This is not the case with Cardsbolt and whenever I add search functionality, it's going to be dead simple. It weirds me out a little bit. As I add more and more shops and more products which might be difficult to get, I'm going to enter a situation where it is better to use Cardsbolt to find the niche stuff. I'm excited for this.
